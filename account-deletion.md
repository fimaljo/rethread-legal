# Rethread Account Deletion

Rethread allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data directly from within the app.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the **Rethread** app.
2. Navigate to **Profile**.
3. Select **Account**.
4. Tap **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm the deletion request.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

* Profile information
* Threads
* Tasks
* Notes
* Reminders
* Blockers
* Other user-generated content associated with your account

## Data Retention

After account deletion is confirmed, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, access to your account and associated data cannot be restored.
