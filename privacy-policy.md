# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Rethread** (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Rethread mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

---

## 1. Overview

Rethread is a subscription app for capturing and organizing personal context (notes, threads, people, tasks, and reminders). We are **local-first**: most data stays on your device unless you turn on **cloud backup** or use features that require network access.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account information

When you sign in with **Google**, we receive information from Firebase Authentication, such as:

- Firebase user ID  
- Display name  
- Email address  
- Profile photo URL (if provided by Google)

We use this to identify your account, sync optional cloud backup, and link your subscription across devices.

### 2.2 Content you provide

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process:

- Text notes and thread content  
- Voice recordings (processed on device; recordings are generally **not** uploaded to cloud backup)  
- People, tasks, reminders, and categories you create  
- Profile name and avatar you set in the app  
- Team/user codes if you use team features  

**Local storage:** Most content is stored in an on-device database on your phone.

**Cloud backup (optional):** If you enable backup, this content is stored in **Google Cloud Firestore** under your Firebase user ID.

### 2.3 Subscription information

We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives:

- Anonymous app user ID (and Firebase user ID after you sign in)  
- Purchase history, entitlement status, and product identifiers  
- Device and store-related metadata needed for billing  

We do **not** receive your full payment card number. Payments are processed by **Apple App Store** or **Google Play**.

### 2.4 AI and search features

When you use AI or semantic search features, the Service may send relevant text (and optionally images you attach) to:

- **Google Firebase AI / Gemini** for structuring, Q&A, and extraction  
- **Google Cloud Functions** (e.g. embedding proxy) for vector search  

We send only what is needed for the feature you request. You can avoid cloud AI by not using those features where they require network access.

### 2.5 Technical and diagnostic data

We may collect:

- App version and device type  
- Crash reports (**Firebase Crashlytics**)  
- App integrity signals (**Firebase App Check**)  
- Basic usage needed to operate sync, notifications, and security  

We do not sell your personal information.

---

## 3. How we use information

We use information to:

- Provide and maintain the Service  
- Authenticate you and enforce subscription access  
- Sync optional cloud backup  
- Run AI, search, and reminder features you enable  
- Improve reliability and fix crashes  
- Comply with law and protect against abuse  

---

## 4. How we share information

We share information only with service providers that help us run the Service:

| Provider | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Google Firebase** | Auth, Firestore backup, storage, Crashlytics, App Check, AI |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management |
| **Apple / Google** | In-app purchases and account sign-in |

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger or acquisition (with notice where required).

We do **not** sell your personal information to advertisers.

---

## 5. Data retention

- **On your device:** Until you delete local data or uninstall the app  
- **Cloud backup:** Until you delete your account, turn off backup, or delete data in the app  
- **Subscription records:** As required by stores and RevenueCat for billing and tax  
- **Crash logs:** Retained per Firebase default retention settings  

You can delete local data in the app. Account deletion removes cloud data tied to your Firebase user as described in the app.

---

## 6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data.

In the app you can:

- Turn **cloud backup** on or off  
- Turn **notifications** on or off  
- **Delete local data**  
- **Delete your account** (removes cloud backup and Firebase auth where supported)  
- **Manage or cancel** your subscription in the App Store or Google Play
